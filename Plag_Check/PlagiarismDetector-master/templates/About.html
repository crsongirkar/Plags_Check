About Plag Check

Plag Check is committed to upholding academic and professional integrity by combating plagiarism. Plagiarism involves the unethical act of utilizing someone else's work, ideas, or intellectual content without proper acknowledgment, thereby presenting it as one's own. Our mission is to provide effective tools and educational resources to promote originality and prevent the consequences of plagiarism.
